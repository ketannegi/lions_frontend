Computer Education and Smart Classes

The computer lab of the school has the latest multimedia machines for providing hands on experience to students from class I -- class XII. The concept of digital learning is also introduced in the school from class Pre-School to Class X in collaboration with Tata Class Edge. The classrooms are well equipped with digital boards and projectors for the live lessons.

Lions Public School is a co-educational school from Nursery to XII. It prepares the students for the senior school (XII) & the secondary school (X) certificate of the Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E).
