The School functioning in the year 1969 at Tri Nagar & shifted to present premises at I-Block, Ashok Vihar, Phase -1, Delhi. The foundation stone of the present structure was laid in 1972 by the President Hon. Shri V.V Giri. Since then the school has made tremendous progress. In the first instance the school was recognized up to primary level, in the year 1995 it was upgraded to Middle, in 1997 to secondary in 2003 for Sr. Secondary level. It is recognized by the Delhi administration & affiliated to C.B.S.E. the student are prepared for All India Senior Secondary Board Examination conducted by C.B.S.E.

Lions Public School is a co-educational English medium school dedicated to the cause of progress education leading to augmentation of the anlytical facilities of the fleding minds, broadening their vision in every spehere of life, be it studies, sports, culture & estra curricular activities. Our education policy is dynamic in approach and in consonance with the changing needs of the society. It seeks to foster students to develop efficiencey to face challenges of the fast changing society. The managing Committee consists of men of eminent stature & educationist and are dedicated to promotion of sound moral, intellectual and physical education.

Our endeavor is to make entire process of learning so interesting that the students fall in love with the school. Modern and latest teaching techniques, discipline through example and encouragement, excellent failities for co-curricular and sporting activities make the school an ideal platform for physical, mental, emotional and spiritual growth of the kids in its fold.

MISSION

Empowering Enriching and Enlightenment of our school community for their holistic development.

VISION

To make the institution par excellence from academic & co curricular stand point where young minds are moulded and ignited with quest for learning, spirit of innovation and scientific temperament.

AIMS & OBJECTIVES

Our aim is to provide a holistic, quality education for students, so that they can develop their mind, body and soul.

We believe that each child is a unique individual with a variety of talents and learning needs. Students are trained for life, to be high achievers and good human beings. A habit of goal setting time management and selfless service is encouraged, which fosters in the students an attitude & outlook, that is necessary for taping on to new knowledge in the process facing the challenges of life.

Besides imparting education in the commonly accepted courses, we at Lions Public School also endeavor to:

Impart education to all in an environment of purity moral & ethical values , with special focus on character building.

Develop all round personality through good education.

Develop integrity and versatility of character.

Provide training in leadership and self governance through the students Council and other allied positions of responsibility.

Develop creative potential of children by encouraging self expression of thought through a variety of media like art, music, dance, drama, and brain storming sessions etc.

Promote national integration, foster international understanding, and develop respect and understanding of different beliefs, ideas and cultures.

Creating an awareness among the learners for maintaining environmental purity and ecological harmony.

CURRICULUM

"The goal of education is the advancement of knowledge and the dissemination of truth.” The School follows the recommendations of the NCERT & prepares children for examinations conducted by C.B.S.E. The school has adopted the three language formula English is taught as first language whereas Hindi & Sanskrit being second & third language. Environmental Science, Computer Science, Mathematics, Social Science Music & Art Education form the subjects of the core-curriculum up to the Secondary stage. At the Senior Secondary stage provision is made for English, Hindi, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Mathematics, Computer Science, Informatics Practices, Physical & Health Education, Economics, Accountancy, Business Studies, Political Science & History. Facilities are available for learning Music, Art, Sport Judo etc. Our Students actively participate in different inter-School, Zonal state District level Competitions and bring laurels.

WITHDRAWAL

Written notice of one calendar month must be given to withdraw a student from the school or a month's fee will be charged in lieu of notice.

The Principal reserves the right to cancel the admission of a student whose performance and progress in studies is unsatisfactory or whose conduct is harmful to the interest and prestige of the institution.

The name of the student who fails in the same class twice will automatically be removed from the school rolls

Any student caught using unfair means during an examination will not remain on the school rolls.

In case a student stops attending the school without any prior information to the school authorities, his/her name will be struck off the school rolls after six consecutive days of absence.

CODE OF CONDUCT

Students must come in proper School Uniform.

Students must reach the School atleast 5 minutes before the time fixed for assembly.

Students who come late or who have been absent on the previous day must bring an application from the parents.

Irregular attendance, habitual illness, neglect of home work, disobedience and disrespect towards teachers & members of the staff or bad moral influence justify dismissals. Pupils are responsible to the school authorities for their conduct both in and outside school.

In case "of long leave, the guardian of the student himself should" get the leave sanctioned by the Principal beforehand. Any sick leave otherwise during the examination should be supported by a Medical Certificate.

Students should take utmost care of the school property and premises. Any kind of damage done to the school property will lead to fine.

Parents are expected to co-operate in the working of the school enforcing regularity and discipline & taking general interest in their child's progress.

The parents are welcome to meet the Class Teacher & discuss their ward's progress in class & any other problem regarding the child during the Parent-Teacher meet which is held from time to time.

Lions Public School is a co-educational school from Nursery to XII. It prepares the students for the senior school (XII) & the secondary school (X) certificate of the Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E).
